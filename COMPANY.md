## SOPs (Standard Operating Procedures)

### Onboarding a New Account
1. Add the practice to Copper CRM (company + key contacts + opportunity)
2. Set opportunity stage to "Contacted" (or appropriate stage)
3. Confirm ordering method preference (scanning, plaster, biofoam)
4. If scanning: confirm device compatibility, share app link and tutorial
5. Confirm access to Doctor Portal: https://orthoticsportal.com/
6. Walk through first order if needed
7. Follow up after first order delivery (see ONBOARDING.md — Stage 6)

### Handling a Complaint or Remake Request
1. Listen fully — don't get defensive
2. Confirm the issue (wrong size, wrong material, patient discomfort, etc.)
3. Invoke the 6-month adjustment policy when applicable
4. Log the issue in Copper as an activity

6. Follow up with the doctor once resolved
