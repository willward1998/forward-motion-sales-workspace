## Typography

### Primary Font: Gotham
- **Black** — Headlines, hero text
- **Bold** — Subheadings, emphasis
- **Medium** — Buttons, labels
- **Regular** — Body text

### Secondary Font: Helvetica Neue
- **Bold** — Subheadings (alt)
- **Medium** — UI elements
- **Regular** — Body text (alt)
- **Light** — Captions, fine print

## Image Generation Guidance

When generating branded images for Forward Motion Medical:
- Use FM Red (#EA2427) and FM Blue (#3383D7) as primary brand colors
- Keep backgrounds clean — white (#FFFFFF) or light gray (#F7F7F7)
- Professional, clinical aesthetic — trustworthy and modern
- Avoid overly playful or casual visuals
- Product shots: clean white backgrounds, studio lighting
- Marketing visuals: use brand reds and blues as accent colors, not overwhelming
