## Priority Products (Most Used in Marketing)

### Glider ★
- **Category:** Custom Orthotic — Athletic
- **Image:** `images/Product Images/Orthotics/Glider_reference.jpg`
- **Top cover (faces the foot):** Plain blue vinyl. Simple, smooth.
- **Bottom cover (sits in the shoe):** Plain gray suede.
- **Shape:** Full-length insole. Simple, clean, not bulky.
- **Key visual cues:** Blue top, gray bottom. That's it. Keep it simple — do not add extra textures, patterns, or details.

### FM Functional ★
- **Category:** Custom Orthotic — Functional
- **Image:** `images/Product Images/Orthotics/FM Functional_reference.jpg`
- **Shell:** Black PRX Graphite rigid shell with a distinctive raised honeycomb/dimpled texture on the bottom. Very rigid-looking.
- **Top cover:** Copper/bronze Marine Vinyl, smooth leather-like texture with a slight sheen.
- **Shape:** Shell only (sulcus length — ends before the toes). Deep 12mm heel cup. White edge layer visible between shell and top.
- **Key visual cues:** The black honeycomb-textured bottom and copper/bronze top is unmistakable. Shorter than full-length devices — no forefoot extension.

### Dress Elite ★
- **Category:** Custom Orthotic — Dress
- **Image:** `images/Product Images/Orthotics/Dress Elite_reference.jpg`
- **Shell:** Dark chocolate brown with a smooth surface.
- **Top cover:** Brown/cognac Marine Vinyl on the face-down device, dark brown Ultra Suede-like cover on the face-up device.
- **Shape:** Full-length, narrow profile designed for dress shoes. 10mm cup (lower than standard). Slim, elegant profile.
- **Key visual cues:** All-brown color scheme — darker brown suede top, lighter cognac shell visible from side. Hole in heel with Poron fill (subtle). Narrower than athletic devices.

### Gauntlet ★
- **Category:** Custom AFO Brace — BOSS Gauntlet
- **Image:** `images/Product Images/AFOs/Gauntlet_reference.jpg`
- **Exterior:** Black leather covering, smooth and polished with visible stitching along seams.
- **Closure:** Black lace-up front with metal eyelets, boot-style construction.
- **Interior:** White leather/vinyl lining visible at the toe opening.
- **Shape:** 5" height ankle brace, solid-ankle design (no hinge). Boot-like silhouette that wraps around the foot and ankle. Open-toe design.
- **Key visual cues:** Looks like a short black leather lace-up boot. White interior contrasts sharply with black exterior. No straps — laces only.

### Balance Brace ★
- **Category:** Custom AFO Brace — BOSS Gauntlet
- **Image:** `images/Product Images/AFOs/Balance-Brace_reference.jpg`
- **Exterior:** Black leather covering, smooth finish.
- **Closure:** Two wide velcro/hook-and-loop straps around the ankle area.
- **Interior:** White lining visible at the toe opening and inside.
- **Shape:** Low-profile, 5" height. Shorter and more compact than the Gauntlet. Open-toe. Wraps around hindfoot and ankle but sits lower than the Gauntlet.
- **Key visual cues:** Black leather with two horizontal velcro straps (not laces). More compact/sleek than the Gauntlet. White interior visible at front opening.
