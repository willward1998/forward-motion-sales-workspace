# Product Knowledge — Forward Motion Medical

## Company Overview
Forward Motion Medical (commonly called "Forward Motion") makes custom orthotics for podiatrist offices. The company's clients are doctor's offices — not patients directly. They have CPeds and techs with 40+ years of experience are on staff and available for consultations.

---

## Orthotic Types

### Functional / Rigid
- Controls abnormal foot motion
- Used for: plantar fasciitis, posterior tibial tendon dysfunction, overpronation
- Shell: typically polypropylene

### Semi-Rigid / Gllider
- The most popular devices ordered out of Forward Motion
- Balances control with cushioning
- Used for: general foot pain, patients who need custom yet tolerable device
- Shell: Performance Rx Semi Rigid 

### Pediatric
- Sized and shaped for children's feet
- Used for: flatfoot, gait abnormalities, in-toeing/out-toeing

### Athletic / Sport-Specific
- Designed for activity-specific stress patterns
- Used for: runners, athletes, high-impact patients

### Diabetic / Offloading
- Prioritizes pressure redistribution and skin protection
- Used for: diabetic patients, wound care, ulcer prevention

## Casting / Capture Methods

### Plaster Cast
- Traditional method; mold of the foot is taken in the office
- Shipped to the lab in a box
- Turnaround starts when the cast arrives at the lab

### Biofoam Box
- Patient steps into a foam impression box
- Box is shipped to the lab
- Turnaround starts when the box arrives

### 3D Scanning (Most Popular)
Scanning allows for faster turnaround and is the most common method used by Forward Motion doctors.

**Compatible hardware:**
- iPhone with TrueDepth sensor (Iphone 12 or newer)
- iPad Pro (Facial recognition)
- iPad + Structure Sensor

**App:** Forward Motion Medical app (free)
- iOS: https://apps.apple.com/us/app/forward-motion-medical/id1169056512

**Tutorials:**
- Scanning Tutorial: https://youtu.be/8JfNTzzXtqI
- Brace Scanning Tutorial: https://youtu.be/-WZ9LQCo93M

**How to onboard a new office to scanning:**
1. Confirm they have a compatible device
2. Have them download the Forward Motion Medical app (free, App Store)
3. Share the scanning tutorial video
4. Offer to walk them through the first scan or schedule a quick demo

## Ordering Process

### How to Place an Order
- Orders can be tracked through the Doctor Portal: https://orthoticsportal.com/
- Casting (plaster/biofoam) is shipped to the lab; scanning goes directly through the app/portal

### Turnaround Time
- Standard: **2 to 2.5 weeks** from when the cast/scan arrives at the lab
- Scanning typically results in faster turnaround than physical casts

### Change Window Policy
- **Within 48 hours of placing the order:** changes are free
- **After 48 hours:** changes cost **$35**

## Adjustments and Remakes

### 6-Month Adjustment Policy
- Any device that is wrong or causes issues for the patient can be remade correctly within 6 months
- This is a key differentiator — doctors don't need to worry about eating the cost if something needs to be corrected
- We will remake the device from scratch the no need to make trips to the post office just to ship orders back. Remakes can be made through the app.

### Refurbishment
- We can refurbish orthotics that have worn out
- $65 for a device not originally made by Forward Motion
- $35 for a Forward Motion-made device
