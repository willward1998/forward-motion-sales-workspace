# Products & Pricing — Forward Motion Medical

## Company Overview
Forward Motion Medical (commonly called "Forward Motion") makes custom orthotics, AFO braces, and prefabricated orthotics for podiatrist offices. The company's clients are doctor's offices — not patients directly. On staff we have CPeds and techs with 40+ years of experience are on staff and available for consultations.

## Product Line Overview

Forward Motion offers three product families:

1. **Custom Orthotics** ($99) — 18 named devices across 7 categories, plus 20 condition-specific configurations. All custom-molded from casts, foam impressions, or 3D scans.
2. **Custom AFO Braces** ($229–$599) — 11 ankle-foot orthosis devices in the BOSS Gauntlet, Thermoplastic, and Specialty lines.
3. **Prefabricated Orthotics** ($9.95–$29.95) — 20+ ready-made insoles, shells, and children's devices across 6 series. Shipping charges apply.

## Casting / Capture Methods

### Plaster Cast
- Traditional method; mold of the foot is taken in the office
- Shipped to the lab in a box
- Turnaround starts when the cast arrives at the lab

### Biofoam Box
- Patient steps into a foam impression box
- Box is shipped to the lab
- Turnaround starts when the box arrives

### 3D Scanning (Most Popular)
Scanning allows for faster turnaround and is the most common method used by Forward Motion doctors.

**Compatible hardware (recommended first):**
- iPhone with TrueDepth sensor (iPhone 12 or newer)
- iPad Pro with LiDAR/TrueDepth
- iPad + Structure Sensor (also supported)

**App:** Forward Motion Medical app (free)
- iOS: https://apps.apple.com/us/app/forward-motion-medical/id1169056512

**Tutorials:**
- Scanning Tutorial: https://youtu.be/8JfNTzzXtqI
- Brace Scanning Tutorial: https://youtu.be/-WZ9LQCo93M

**How to onboard a new office to scanning:**
1. Confirm they have a compatible device
2. Have them download the Forward Motion Medical app (free, App Store)
3. Share the scanning tutorial video
4. Offer to walk them through the first scan or schedule a quick demo

## Ordering Process

### How to Place an Order
- Orders can be tracked through the Doctor Portal: https://orthoticsportal.com/
- Casting (plaster/biofoam) is shipped to the lab; scanning goes directly through the app/portal

### Turnaround Time
- Standard: **2 to 2.5 weeks** from when the cast/scan arrives at the lab
- Scanning typically results in faster turnaround than physical casts

### Change Window Policy
- **Within 48 hours of placing the order:** changes are free
- **After 48 hours:** $35 for orthotics, $50 for braces

---

## 6-Month Adjustment Policy

Any device that is wrong or causes issues for the patient can be remade correctly within 6 months — at no cost. Doctors don't need to worry about eating the cost if something needs to be corrected. We remake the device from scratch; no need to ship the old pair back. Remakes can be made through the app.
